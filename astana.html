About Nur-Sultan

Nur-Sultan is the capital of Kazakhstan and the newest, most modern and fashionable city in the country. People come here to look at unusual architectural forms. The city is located on the Ishim River, which divides it into the right and left banks. The left bank is more beautiful - it is here that all the unusual buildings are located.It is full of magnificent attractions, educational institutions, hotels, office buildings and other commercial structures. It boasts well-developed transport infrastructure and rather favourable environment.
